A short flagcat history

Flagcat is a daily game that was originally conceived by my friend anderson. The challenge was created using the /flag command from esmbot on top of The flagcat.

Friends of theirs were tempted to take the challenge through anderson's discord status, which read: "Ask me for today's flag cat!"

We decided to work together to bring flagcat to a grander stage, as word of mouth can only travel so far.

Some notes on mentality

At the end of the day, flagcat is about having fun. We understand that it's only human to lose ourselves in the heat of passion, but it's still important to do what you can to keep a healthy mindset!
